Who I am

Someone wired to
connect the dots.

I'm Karlita — a linguist, content creator, dancer, choreographer, and academic tutor based in Bandung, Indonesia. I graduated from Universitas Widyatama with a focus on Critical Discourse Analysis, and I've spent years trying to understand one thing: how language shapes the way we see ourselves.

Off the page, I direct and produce dance productions with my group TEQUILA — handling everything from choreography and audio mixing to the overall creative vision. I coach and teach formally, because I believe how you pass knowledge on matters just as much as the knowledge itself. And yes — I play games. Deeply, curiously, and with the same attention I bring to everything else.

Along the way, I've had the quiet privilege of competing — and winning — at national and international levels in poetry, writing, and English. But more than the trophies, what stays with me is what competing taught me: how to perform under pressure, how to read a room, and how to make words land.

Underneath all of it, there's one thread — human transformation. Helping people become their best selves, without losing who they are. That's not a career path. That's a calling.
